The "Deep South" is considered to be a major stronghold for conservatives and conservative values. Candidates running for office in these areas really have to understand what their typical voter is like. Demographics can play a key role in understanding why certain districts vote for a particular candidate. The following data visualization tool uses demographic information taken from the US census and 2016 election results from the Cook Political Report's 2017 political report partisan voter index.
